# Rank and Rent Compliance — Los Angeles CA 1031 Exchange Content

## Core Principles

All content must prioritize educational value over promotional claims. Users should leave with accurate information about 1031 exchanges, not pressure to act immediately.

## Language Standards

### Never Claim Past Projects or Clients
**❌ Wrong:**
"We helped a Beverly Hills investor save $2M in taxes last year."

**✅ Right:**
"Example of the type of engagement we can handle: A Beverly Hills luxury property owner seeking tax-deferred reinvestment."

### Frame Scenarios as Examples
**❌ Wrong:**
"Our client sold a downtown LA office building and reinvested in multifamily."

**✅ Right:**
"Situations we handle include downtown Los Angeles office building owners considering multifamily reinvestment."

### Professional Reference Policy
**❌ Wrong:**
"Call for a reference from our satisfied client in Pasadena."

**✅ Right:**
"We can share references upon request. Contact us to discuss your situation in Pasadena, CA."

## Regulatory Compliance

### IRS Resources Integration
- Link to IRS Like Kind Exchanges (Section 1031) page
- Reference IRS Form 8824 instructions
- Link to Rev Proc 2008 16 for vacation homes
- Include state-specific tax authority links

### Tax Deferral Accuracy
**Always Include:**
"1031 defers income tax on qualifying real property and does not remove transfer or documentary taxes."

**Never Claim:**
- "Save on all taxes"
- "Eliminate capital gains"
- Specific tax savings amounts
- Guaranteed outcomes

### Qualified Intermediary References
**✅ Acceptable:**
- "Engage a qualified intermediary with segregated accounts"
- "Work with QI firms that provide fidelity insurance"
- "Coordinate with bonded escrow providers"

**❌ Prohibited:**
- "We provide QI services"
- "Our affiliated QI firm"
- Specific QI firm recommendations

## Content Boundaries

### What We Can Say
- Educational content about 1031 rules
- Property identification assistance
- Timeline management education
- General market information
- Regulatory requirement explanations

### What We Cannot Say
- Tax advice or opinions
- Legal advice or opinions
- Investment recommendations
- Specific financial planning
- Success rate claims
- Performance guarantees

**❌ Prohibited:**
